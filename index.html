Caring for Orchids: A Visual Narrative of Female Genital Mutilation

Just as orchids defy the odds and flourish in the most challenging of environments, so too do individuals impacted by the practice of female genital mutilation (FGM). This data story illustrates the harms of FGM using orchids as metaphors.

Which Way Will the World Vote?

Around 64 countries are holding their elections in 2024. This project scrapes the voter turnout from each election monitored by the International Foundation for Electoral Systems website using Python, Playwright, and BeautifulSoup. A diverging bar chart is then used to show which way each country voted: progressive or conservative.

Biden's Judicial Nominees

As of December 2023, U.S. President Joe Biden has nominated 140 individuals in the federal district court. More than half of the appointed judges are women. This map shows names of the individuals Biden nominated for each judicial district as of December 2023. Data was scraped from the White House website using Python, BeautifulSoup and regex.
